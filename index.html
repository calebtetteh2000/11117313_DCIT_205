Undergraduate Programmes

Open the door to sought-after technology careers with a world-class Bachelor of Science in Computer Science degree. The Department offers two undergraduate degree programmes, Bachelor of Science in Computer Science Single-Major (3:2:1:1) and Major-Minor (3:2:2:1) as well as Bachelor of Science in Information Technology.

MPhil and Msc Programmes

Embrace world class graduate education with unique blend of cutting-edge research and industry experience. The Department of Computer Science - University of Ghana currently runs Masters of Science (MSc.) in Computer Science and Master of Philosophy (MPhil.) in Computer Science.

PhD Programmes

Our PhD's lead the effort to extend the boundaries of knowledge through quality and industry relevant research. Our doctoral programme is a full-time research programme with expected duration of 4-years. For the successful completion of this programme, a high level of commitment is required. Students need to live close to the University and be a full-time participant in the program.
